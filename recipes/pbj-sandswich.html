Peanut Butter Jelly Sandwich

Description

For this recipe, we will make a peanut butter sandwich

Ingredients

  • Peanut butter
  • Jelly
  • Sandwich bread

Steps

  1. Take Two Slices of Bread
  2. Spread Peanut Butter Onto One Slice of Bread
  3. Spread the Jelly Onto the Other Slice of Bread
  4. Lay Two Sides Together
  5. (Optional) Cut Crusts
  6. (Optional) Cut Into Half
  7. Dig In!
